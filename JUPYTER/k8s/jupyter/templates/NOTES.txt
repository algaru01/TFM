
To access Jupyter Notebook Server you must first know Ingress IP:

kubectl get ingress jupyter-cluster-frontend-ingress -o jsonpath='{.status.loadBalancer.ingress[0].ip}'

And then the token generated in the Frontend:

kubectl logs jupyter-cluster-frontend | grep -o 'token=[a-f0-9]*' | head -n 1 | awk -F= '{print $2}'

Now, you can acces via HTTP:

http:<ingress-ip>/tree?token=<token>
